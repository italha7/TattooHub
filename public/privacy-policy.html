PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Tattoo Hub ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website at tattoohub.onrender.com.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from visitors. Our website is a read-only tattoo gallery and does not require registration or login from general users.

We may collect anonymous usage data such as page views and browser type through standard server logs.

2. Pinterest Integration

Our website uses the Pinterest API to display publicly available tattoo content from Pinterest boards. We only access public Pinterest data and do not store any Pinterest user credentials or private information.

Content displayed on our website is sourced from public Pinterest boards. Pinterest's own privacy policy applies to all Pinterest content and interactions on their platform.

3. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. No personal data is stored in cookies.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

— Pinterest API: To fetch and display public tattoo content. Subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy at pinterest.com/privacy-policy.

— Render.com: For website hosting. Subject to Render's Privacy Policy at render.com/privacy.

— MongoDB Atlas: For database storage. Subject to MongoDB's Privacy Policy at mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Storage

We store only publicly available tattoo content fetched from Pinterest. No personal user data is stored in our database.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through our Pinterest page at pinterest.com/tattoo_hub.
